Defining an extension

Creating an extension for the Computation Wizard extension point is fairly easy. This help page explains the process step by step, but you can also take a look at the schema.

  • Open the plugin.xml file.
  • On the Dependencies tab: add net.bioclipse.compute to your plug-in.
  • On the Extensions tab: add net.bioclipse.compute.computationWizards to the tree.
  • Add wizards, primary wizards and categories as specified in the schema.
