Jmol Editor

The Jmol Editor is used to visualize molecules in 3D. It requires that the molecule has 3D coordinates to open. When opened, a new top menu is available named Jmol.



Click and hold the left mouse button in the view to rotate the protein. Use the mouse wheel to zoom in and out, alt hold down SHIFT and click + drag at the same time.
